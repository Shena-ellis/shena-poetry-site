Deep Definitive Decision

  1. Home
  2. Poetry
  3. Deep Definitive Decision
I Hope this Carries a Conclusion
Causing Closure of Delusion
Deep Definitive Decision
Ditching Dire Disposition

Maybe I Forgot to Mention
That this Butterfly's Infection
Causes Constant Recollection

I should Keep My Urge Protected
Satisfy With What I Get
Instead of Chasing You Away
When All I Want is You Around

So I Swirl this Southbound Spiral
Stuck Inside Some Sweet Denial
Force Your Friendship For A Smile
Driving Mile After Mile

Showing Up to be Shut Down
You Seem Good when I'm Not Around
I Lust to Loose this Lost and Found
You're Fucking Every Girl In Town

Forget Me Not, Fair-Weathered Friend
When Once Comes Our Acquaintence's End
Returning Every Note You Send
That You Don't Miss Me, You'll Pretend
