History

Childhood

The formative years

  • Born in Stockton, CA in 1984 to Mother Shelene (age 14) and Father Mike (age 21)
  • In 1990, Mike gains full custody; Mom takes me and my little brother (4 years my junior) to Washington
  • 1994: Mom is arrested for child abduction and I am sent back to CA to live with Mike, a total stranger. Mom is sent to prison for 2 years for child abduction.

High School

Edison Senior High School

1999–2002
  • Dr. Martin Luther King Blvd. (formerly Charter Way), Stockton, CA
  • Fighting my way through high school, being the target in gang initiations, and blamed during riots for the deportation of a student involved in a fight, I still managed to maintain a 3.6 GPA.

Big Sur, CA

Gorda Springs Resort

Originally 2012

When I was 27, a friend called me one day and said “I just got you a job and a place to live on the coast.” No questions asked, I packed 1 suitcase and left Stockton, and never looked back.

Gorda Springs Resort

  • Hired based on recommendation – I was 27 years old
  • On-site housing
  • Worked 6 days a week at the General Store
  • Watched whales breach for over an hour with Hilary Swank on the front porch in front of the store, never even asking her name. It wasn’t until after she left that my boss came running over and excitedly explained who she was!

My Hero

David Toschak

2019–present

  • Started dating June 20, 2019
  • Moved to Palo Colorado Rd. in 2020
  • He proposed on January 18, 2022
  • He remains my full-time caregiver, my salvation, my Hero

The Crash

Becoming a Quadriplegic

Monday, May 23, 2022
  • Driving a 1999 Porsche Boxster Convertible (5-speed manual)
  • On my way home from work, I flipped a tiny convertible going 65mph on Highway 1
  • Was airlifted to Natividad Hospital in Salinas, arriving over 4 hours after my accident
  • Broke my neck at the C5–C6 level, compacting my spine 4 inches and severing nerves
  • Shattered the left side of my face and suffered a traumatic brain injury which caused me to stutter for 4 months
  • David never left my side
  • Check out My Hero for more about this amazing man!
